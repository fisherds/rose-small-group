Rose-Hulman The Story Small Group

Print the flyer and help spread the word

Group Leader: Dave Fisher

What: A “Small Group” is a group of people that meet weekly to talk about life from a Christian perspective. Specifically, in this group, we will be discussing one chapter per week of the book “The Story”. Everyone that joins will get a book, which is free for students and only $5 for faculty and staff.

Who: Anyone, Christian or non-Christian, students, faculty, or staff, that has interest is learning more about what is in the bible. It can be difficult to read the whole bible, but The Story is an abridged bible that is much easier to read because it is written in a narrative form. With the help of others, you can be successful in reading all of the stories in the bible!

When: Each Friday at noon

  • Sept 2nd - Open House to learn about what this group is (free cookie!)
  • Sept 9th - Book Distribution & Group Intro
  • Sept 16th - Chapter 1
  • Sept 23rd - Chapter 2
  • … up to Chapter 31

Where: Location TBD (email Dave Fisher)

Why: To take one step closer to Christ.



Interested?

Come to the Open House on Sept 2nd anytime between noon to 12:50pm. Come introduce yourself. See a book. Let me know that you are interested. No commitment if you come to the Open House, just ask questions and eat a cookie. We want people to succeed in their goal to read the bible and fellowship with others!
